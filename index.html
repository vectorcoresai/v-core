What is V-CORE?

V-CORE is a trend-regime engine that sits underneath the VectorCoresAI stack. Instead of trying to pick entries for you, it focuses on what kind of market you are currently in.

  • Colour-coded regimes (bull, bear, neutral) based on a combination of market conditions.
  • Designed as a context layer that trading ideas, risk and management can plug into.
  • Built first on crypto, then extended across FX, indices and stocks in the Pro editions.

Free V-CORE releases give a locked-down view of that regime logic. Pro editions expose additional states, more flexible behaviour and tighter integration into different workflows.

V-CORE Pro Engine & Signal Governor

V-CORE Engine Pro

The professional engine used internally and with early testers. It is designed to read market structure in a consistent, repeatable way across crypto, FX, indices and stocks.

  • Additional internal regime states beyond simple bull / bear / neutral.
  • Behaviour on stocks and indices is tuned for cleaner rotations and less visual noise.
  • Forex behaviour focuses on how directional moves build, compress and release over time.

Engine Pro does not promise profitable trades. It aims to provide a stable bias map so timing and management decisions feel more deliberate.

The Signal Governor (‚ÄúThe Gov‚Äù)

The Gov sits on top of the engine as a classification and filtering layer. It helps highlight sections of market behaviour that are more or less favourable for trade ideas.

  • Tags opportunities into categories such as stronger, standard or lower-quality zones.
  • Flags environments that often align with thin liquidity or unstable volatility.
  • Built to sit alongside manual workflows, bots or external execution systems.

Early internal feedback describes the Gov as a way to separate ideas that deserve focus from those that are better treated as background noise.
